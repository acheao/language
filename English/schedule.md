## Simple Weekly Routine
### Daily
1. Review one short source note.
2. Save new words into [vocabulary](<./material/other/vocabulary.md>).
3. Write one sentence or speak one short summary out loud.

### Three Times Per Week
1. Review one grammar topic from [Grammar Index](<./grammar/Index.md>).
2. Find one real example from a source note.

### Weekly
1. Pick one deeper note from [TED](<./material/TED/Index.md>) or [CNN](<./material/CNN/Index.md>).
2. Summarize it in your own English.
3. Add weak points to the right section of this vault.

## How To Choose Material
- Low energy: pick `English with Lucy` or `Vanessa`
- Want current topics: pick `CNN`
- Want deep listening and speaking ideas: pick `TED`
- Want technical English: pick [freeCodeCamp Notes](<./freeCodeCamp.org/Index.md>)
